_Effective Date: 15 October 2025_

This Privacy Policy explains how InnerShift collects, uses, and protects your personal information.
#### 1. Information I Collect
I may collect the following types of information when you use this website or book a session:
Name and contact information (email, phone)
Booking details (session type, time, etc.)
Payment information (handled securely through third-party payment processors)
Voluntary information shared through forms or email communication

#### 2. How Your Information Is Used
Your personal information is used to:
Schedule and manage coaching sessions
Send newsletters and relevant content (if you subscribe)
Process payments (through secure third-party processors)
Improve my website and services

#### 3. Data Storage and Security
Your personal data is stored securely and only for as long as necessary.
I use trusted third-party providers (e.g., scheduling and payment platforms) who maintain their own security standards.

#### 4. Sharing of Information
I will never sell or rent your personal information.
Your data may be shared only when required to:
Deliver the services you’ve requested (e.g., scheduling systems, payment processors)
Comply with legal obligations

#### 5. Cookies and Analytics
This website may use cookies and analytics to understand traffic and improve the user experience. You can adjust cookie preferences in your browser at any time.

#### 6. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
Access your personal data
Request corrections or deletion
Withdraw consent to communications
File a complaint with a data protection authority

#### 7. Email Communication
You may unsubscribe from the InnerShift newsletter or other communications at any time by clicking “unsubscribe” in any email or contacting me directly.

#### 8. Contact
For questions about this Privacy Policy, [contact me](../contact/).
