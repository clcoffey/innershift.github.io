## General SEO Best Practices

### âœ… **Do:**
- Link naturally within sentences, not as list items only
- Use 1-3 internal links per 1000 words
- Link contextually (same topic cluster)
- Use descriptive anchor text that hints at the linked content
- Link from high-traffic posts to newer or underperforming posts

### âŒ **Don't:**
- Force links that don't make sense
- Use generic anchor text like "read more" or "click here"
- Link to the same post repeatedly in one article
- Create orphaned posts with no internal links
