## Link Placement Guidelines

### ‚úÖ **Best Practices:**
- Place links **within a sentence or paragraph**, not in bullet points
- Link when the topic is **directly relevant**
- Use 1-2 links per 500 words
- Link **contextually** to the same theme cluster

### Example:
```markdown
‚ùå Bad:
When burnout shows up, your nervous system responds. 
- See: Success Mask post for more details
- Read about: Herd Morality

‚úÖ Good:
When burnout shows up, your nervous system is responding to years of 
[wearing a success mask](link) or living according to [herd morality](link) 
instead of your authentic truth.
```

## Testing Your Links

After implementing links:
1. **Build your Hugo site locally:** `hugo server`
2. **Check all links work** by clicking through
3. **Verify link text is descriptive** (not generic)
4. **Test on mobile** to ensure readability
